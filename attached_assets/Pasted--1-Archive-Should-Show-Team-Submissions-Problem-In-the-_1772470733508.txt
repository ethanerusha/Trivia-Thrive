
1️⃣ Archive Should Show Team Submissions
Problem:
In the archive, teams can only see correct answers. They cannot see what they submitted.
Requirements:
	• In the archive view for a past week: 
		○ Show: 
			§ Question
			§ Correct answer
			§ The logged-in user’s team submission
			§ Whether that submission was correct
	• This should only show that team’s own submissions.
	• Do not expose other teams’ answers.
	• If a team did not submit, display: "No submission".
Update backend queries and frontend rendering accordingly.
